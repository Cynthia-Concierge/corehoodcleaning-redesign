Pacific Beach Fire Code Compliance & Health Inspections

Pacific Beach falls under San Diego Fire-Rescue Department Station 21 jurisdiction. PB restaurants face frequent health inspections due to the high density of food establishments in the Garnet Avenue corridor and beachfront areas.

What Pacific Beach Health Inspectors Check:

  • Current hood cleaning certificate showing service within NFPA 96 required intervals
  • Before/after photos (easily accessed via your customer portal)
  • Clean baffle filters with no grease dripping or saturation
  • Hood interior free of excessive grease buildup
  • Proper ductwork access panels for full-system cleaning
  • Working exhaust fan with adequate CFM for cooking volume
  • Fire suppression system with current annual inspection tag
