Carlsbad Fire Code Compliance & Health Inspections

Carlsbad falls under San Diego Fire-Rescue Department Station 5 jurisdiction. Carlsbad restaurants face rigorous health inspections from San Diego County Health & Human Services. The city's upscale dining focus means health inspectors maintain elevated expectations for cleanliness, safety, and NFPA 96 compliance in all food service operations.

What Carlsbad Health Inspectors Check:

  • Current hood cleaning certificate showing service within NFPA 96 required intervals
  • Before/after photos and detailed service documentation
  • Clean baffle filters with no grease dripping or saturation
  • Hood interior free of excessive grease buildup or discoloration
  • Proper ductwork access panels for full-system inspection
  • Working exhaust fan with adequate CFM for cooking volume
  • Fire suppression system with current annual inspection tag
  • Clean and properly functioning makeup air systems
  • No evidence of past fire damage or equipment failure
