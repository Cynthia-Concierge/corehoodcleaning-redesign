Escondido Fire Code Compliance & Health Inspections

Escondido falls under San Diego County Fire-Rescue Department and City of Escondido Fire Marshal jurisdiction. Escondido restaurants face regular health inspections due to the high density of food establishments throughout the city and surrounding North County areas.

What Escondido Health Inspectors Check:

  • Current hood cleaning certificate showing service within NFPA 96 required intervals
