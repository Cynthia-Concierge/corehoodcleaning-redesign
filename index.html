San Diego Filter Exchange Program

Restaurants are ALWAYS asking us if we can provide ongoing filter exchange service, so we decided to make it official.

Every night after service, your team removes the dirty filters from the hood, places them in the filter collection bin, and replaces them with freshly cleaned filters. We swing by once per week, collect the dirty filters, clean them in our shop, and deliver fresh filters back to you.

When is Our New Filter Exchange Program?

You can add filter exchange to any recurring hood cleaning service plan. We'll deliver fresh filters weekly and clean your dirty ones. Your staff never has to scrub another filter.
