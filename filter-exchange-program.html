What is a Restaurant Filter Exchange Program?

A filter exchange program is a scheduled service where clean, sanitized grease filters are swapped out for your dirty filters on a regular basis. Instead of cleaning your own filters (which is time-consuming, messy, and often done incorrectly), professional filters are delivered on a schedule that keeps your kitchen fire-safe and compliant with San Diego fire codes.

Why Grease Filters Are Critical to Fire Safety

Grease filters (also called baffle filters) are the first line of defense in your exhaust system. They're designed to catch grease particles before they enter the ductwork and accumulate as a fire hazard. When filters are saturated with grease, they stop working - allowing grease to bypass the filters and coat your ductwork, plenum, and exhaust fan with highly flammable material.

The problem? Most restaurant staff don't have time to properly clean filters during a busy shift. Even when filters are "cleaned," they're often just rinsed with water (which doesn't remove baked-on grease) or run through a dish machine (which isn't hot enough to dissolve grease). The result: filters that look clean but are still saturated with grease and ineffective at capturing new grease particles.

The solution? A professional filter exchange program. We deliver professionally cleaned filters (soaked in commercial degreaser and pressure washed) on a schedule that matches your cooking volume. Your staff simply swaps out dirty for clean - no scrubbing, no mess, no guesswork.

Why DIY Filter Cleaning Doesn't Work

Many restaurant owners try to save money by having staff clean filters themselves. Here's why that rarely works:

❌ Kitchen Dish Machines Aren't Hot Enough

Grease needs water at 180°F+ with strong degreaser to dissolve. Most dish machines top out at 160°F and use mild detergent - not strong enough for baked-on grease.

❌ Staff Don't Have Time During Service

Properly cleaning filters takes 20-30 minutes of scrubbing with degreaser. During a busy shift, filters get a quick rinse at best - leaving them still saturated with grease.

❌ Manual Scrubbing Misses Hidden Grease

Baffle filters have complex internal channels that trap grease. Only professional pressure washing with heated degreaser solution reaches these hidden areas.

❌ It's Messy, Unpleasant, and Inconsistent

Cleaning filters creates grease splatters, drain clogs, and slippery floors. Quality varies by who's doing it and how rushed they are.

❌ You Still Need Professional Hood Cleaning

Even with perfect filter maintenance, you still need quarterly or monthly full-system hood cleaning. The filter program is an add-on that keeps your system cleaner between professional cleanings.

Frequently Asked Questions About Filter Exchange

Do I still need hood cleaning if I have a filter exchange program?

Yes. Filter exchange keeps filters clean, but you still need professional hood cleaning to address grease buildup in ductwork, plenum, and exhaust fans. Think of filter exchange as preventative maintenance that extends the time between deep cleanings and reduces fire risk between services. Most customers pair monthly or quarterly hood cleaning with weekly or bi-weekly filter exchange.

What happens if my filters are damaged or worn out?

We inspect every filter during cleaning. If we find damage (bent baffles, torn mesh, broken frames), we replace them at no extra charge as part of your program. This ensures you always have functional filters protecting your hood system.

Can I change my exchange frequency if my business changes?

Absolutely. If your cooking volume increases (seasonal changes, new menu items, busier shifts), we can increase your frequency. If volume decreases, we can adjust down. We're flexible - just give us a call to modify your schedule.

What if my filters don't fit standard sizes?

We handle custom filters all the time. Older hoods, imported equipment, and modified systems often have non-standard sizes. During your assessment, we'll measure your exact filter dimensions and either source custom filters or fabricate them to spec.

What time do you deliver filters?

What if I miss a scheduled exchange?

Life happens - unexpected closures, renovations, holidays. If you need to skip or reschedule, just call us. We'll adjust your schedule and deliver when you're ready. No penalties, no fees.

How clean are the filters you deliver?

Professionally cleaned and sanitized. Your filters are soaked in industrial degreaser, pressure washed with heated water, dried, and inspected before delivery. They arrive clean, dry, and ready to install - far cleaner than hand-scrubbing or running through a dish machine.
