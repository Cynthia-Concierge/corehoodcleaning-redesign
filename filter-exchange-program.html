Why Filter Exchange Matters

Your First Line of Defense Against Kitchen Fires

Grease filters are designed to capture grease before it enters your exhaust system. When filters are saturated with grease, they become ineffective - allowing grease to accumulate in your ductwork and creating a serious fire hazard. Regular filter exchanges ensure your filters are always doing their job, protecting your kitchen and keeping you in compliance with fire safety codes. Our program takes the guesswork out of filter maintenance with scheduled service that matches your cooking volume and local fire code requirements.
